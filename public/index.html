ABOUT

Our Mission

Next Generation Tutoring (“NGT”) is a center for educational services. One of our main focuses is individual tutoring to help students strengthen and improve their academic skills, as well as to prepare them for future grade levels and standardized tests.

Tutors and instructors at Next Generation Tutoring are motivated, state-certified educators. We use integrated applications and modern problem solving strategies to help students prepare for 21st century assessments, college and future careers in an increasingly competitive workforce.
